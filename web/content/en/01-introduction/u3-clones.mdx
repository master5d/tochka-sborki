<Phase type="activation">

Where do you lose the most time every week?

Pick one:

- Replying to emails and messages
- Meeting prep and follow-ups
- Creating content (posts, copy, ideas)
- Finding and organizing information
- Repetitive routine tasks

</Phase>

<Phase type="reflection">

Now imagine: someone else does that task. Every day. Well.

What could you be doing instead? Where would your attention go?

That's the whole idea behind **AI clones** — specialized agents for specific jobs.

</Phase>

<Phase type="concept">

## Five types of AI clones

| Clone | What it does | Saves |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Communication** | Replies to emails in your voice | 2–4 h/day |
| **Meeting Intelligence** | Summarizes meetings, writes tasks | 30 min/meeting |
| **Content** | Generates posts, clips, ideas | 5–10 h/week |
| **Learning** | Teaches you new things on a schedule | Quality of life |
| **Automation** | Pipeline: data → report, URL → summary | 5–15 h/week |

**Build cycle:** Capture → Build → Test → Refine.

> 💡 Don't build all five at once. Pick the one where you lose the most time. Meetings 5 and 6 show how to do it technically.

</Phase>

</Phase>
