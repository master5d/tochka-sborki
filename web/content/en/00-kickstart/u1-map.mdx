<Phase type="activation">

Think of a project, app, or automation you've long wanted to build but kept **putting off** — because "it's too hard technically", "I'd need to hire a programmer", or "I don't have six months to learn Python".

**What is that idea?** Describe it in a couple of words.

</Phase>

<Phase type="reflection">

In the old world (before 2024), making this idea real would have cost you:
1.  **3–6 months** learning programming fundamentals.
2.  **Or $2000–$5000** to pay a freelancer.
3.  **Dozens of hours** debugging errors you don't even understand.

**Feel how that barrier kills motivation?** Vibe coding exists to remove it.

</Phase>

<Phase type="concept">

## What is vibe coding?

In plain terms: **you talk — AI writes the code**.

### Difference in approach

| Role | Old development (Software 1.0/2.0) | Vibe coding (Software 3.0) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Who decides WHAT to build** | Product Manager | **YOU** |
| **Who writes the code** | Programmer (3-5 yrs experience) | **AI agent** |
| **Who checks the result** | QA tester | **YOU** |
| **Who ships it to the world** | DevOps engineer | **YOU (with AI)** |

**Your new stack:** You are the conductor. AI is the orchestra. You don't need to play every violin — you need to know the score.

</Phase>

<Phase type="practice">

Open Claude (claude.ai) or any other AI chat.

Write your "ghost idea" and add: "Tell me how you'd help me build this if you were my senior developer. What steps would we take?"

Look at the plan. Save it to `my-experiments/u1-ghost-idea-plan.md`.

</Phase>
